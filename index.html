Same gig different name.
Equiniti Paymaster

We’ve been around for a long time – since 1836 in fact, that’s the year before Queen Victoria came to the throne. We’ve never been afraid of change (we started paying people by hand!) and this year we see our name change to reflect our place in the Equiniti Group.

What won’t be changing is our continued focus on delivering a star performance. As well as being one of the largest pension administrators in the UK, we ensure 2.3 million people receive their pension, look after the closed pension books for some of the largest insurers and provide pension and actuarial resource through Hazell Carr. Together with Equiniti Claybrook we look after over 7 million scheme members in the UK – that’s almost the same size as the population of London! Every great rock star needs a great roadie and we’re here to help your pension scheme members and annuitants receive the VIP service they deserve.
